# Privacy Policy — FocusWave

**Effective date:** 2026

FocusWave is a Chrome extension that generates binaural beats for focus, meditation, relaxation, and sleep. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains — in plain language — exactly what the extension does and does not do with your information.

The short version: **FocusWave collects no personal data, has no backend server, and sends nothing about you anywhere.**

## What We Collect

Nothing.

FocusWave does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no account, no sign-up, and no server that the extension reports to. Everything happens locally on your device.

## Data Stored on Your Device

To remember your preferences between sessions, FocusWave saves a small set of settings locally using the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API. This data lives only on your device and is **never** sent off it. It includes:

- Selected preset (Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta, Gamma)
- Volume level
- Carrier frequency
- Timer setting
- Selected color theme
- Pro license key (if you have entered one)

You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome.

## Analytics, Tracking, and Cookies

FocusWave uses **no** analytics, **no** tracking, **no** cookies, and **no** third-party data sharing of any kind. Your usage is not measured, profiled, or reported.

## Network Requests

FocusWave has no backend and makes no requests that carry your data. The extension may load web fonts (Google Fonts) at load time purely for typography. This is a standard request for font files and contains no personal information; it is unrelated to any feature or setting. No other network requests are made.

## Pro License

The Pro license key is validated **locally, on your device**. Validating or activating Pro does not contact any server and does not transmit your key or any other information anywhere.

## Permissions

FocusWave requests only the minimum permissions needed to function. None of these grant access to your browsing data, history, or the content of any web page.

- **`storage`** — Saves your preferences (preset, volume, carrier frequency, timer, theme, and Pro license key) locally so they persist between sessions.
- **`offscreen`** — Creates an offscreen document that runs the Web Audio engine, so your binaural beats keep playing even when the popup is closed or you switch tabs.
- **`alarms`** — Powers the focus/session timer, allowing the extension to reliably count down and stop playback when time is up.

FocusWave requests no host permissions and cannot read or modify the pages you visit.

## Children's Privacy

FocusWave does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published alongside the extension with a revised effective date.
